About Agra Division

Ensuring every click counts on the tracks. Our AMC Complaint Management System keeps Indian Railways' essential IT hardware – from keyboards to printers – performing flawlessly, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.

With our new Digital Complaint Management System, we ensure prompt resolution of device malfunctions and maintenance issues through our network of registered certified technicians.
